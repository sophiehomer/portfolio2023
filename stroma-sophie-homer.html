Stroma is a medical device company that has developed a laser device designed to permanently change eye color from dark to light. Although the laser is not yet commercially available, there are many inquiries regarding the procedure.

The website was designed and developed in 2016 and was intended to be simple. Instead the UI was overcomplicated, there was large amounts of empty space, it wasn’t completely responsive,  there were two navigations, and both contained confusing verbiage. 

Strōma's new website will be launched in August of 2023.

My Role

Web Design

I facilitated discussions with cross-functional teams and redesigned the website to be simple, straight forward, and better fit user needs. I delivered high fidelity prototypes and worked closely with the developer to work within technical constraints.
