Femme Dept. is an aesthetically designed feminine soap with clean ingredients and a body-positive approach that works towards eliminating the stigmas associated with intimate health. The soap is expertly formulated using all-natural base ingredients to provide a gentle cleanse and nourishment. Femme Dept. aims to offer women an accessible product that fosters confidence and maintains a healthy pH level.

Challenge

The feminine hygiene market has long been plagued by limited options that fail to meet the needs and preferences of women. Existing products often contain harmful chemicals and synthetic fragrances, disrupting the delicate pH balance and leading to irritation. Additionally, the design sensibility of most intimate soaps lacks appeal and fails to align with the desires of women, discouraging bath or shower display. Moreover, many companies lack consciousness in their branding, perpetuating stigmas that fuel body shame and prevent women from proudly embracing their feminine hygiene products.

Research and Insights

Extensive research was conducted, including interviews with women spanning various age groups from 18 to 65, to address these concerns. The findings revealed widespread dissatisfaction with current feminine hygiene products. Women expressed frustration with the harmful ingredients, stigmas surrounding intimate soap, and packaging that perpetuated shame and discomfort.

Solution

The brand's mission is to eradicate stigmas surrounding intimate health while offering women a clean-ingredient, body part-positive, and aesthetically designed soap that caters to their unique needs.
‍
Clean and Nourishing Formula: Femme Dept. soap is carefully formulated with all-natural base ingredients, consciously avoiding harmful chemicals and synthetic fragrances. This formulation guarantees a gentle cleanse that prioritizes comfort and maintains the delicate pH balance of intimate areas. Beyond its cleansing properties, our soap also nourishes, contributing to improved overall intimate health and well-being.

Aesthetically Designed for Empowerment: The brand embraces a thoughtful design approach, offering visually appealing products that women can proudly display. With elegant and modern packaging, Femme Dept. reflects the beauty and sophistication that women deserve, encouraging them to take pride in their self-care routines.
‍
Body-Positive Branding: Femme Dept. actively challenges stigmas and rejects body shame through conscious branding efforts. By celebrating and embracing every aspect of intimate health, Femme Dept. empowers women to feel confident and empowered in their choices. The brand's branding promotes inclusivity, diversity, and self-love, creating a supportive community that uplifts and encourages open conversations.
